# Deck limit reached

On the **Free plan**, Flashycardy limits you to **3 decks**. When you try to create a fourth deck, you see a **Deck limit reached** message instead of the create dialog.

## What you'll see

- A banner on the Dashboard: **X/3 decks used**
- Clicking **New Deck** when you already have 3 decks opens a dialog explaining the Free plan limit
- The dialog includes a **View Plans** button to upgrade

{/* screenshot: deck-limit-reached-dialog */}

## What to do

### Option 1 — Upgrade to Pro

1. Click **View Plans** in the limit dialog, or open **Pricing** from the app header.
2. Subscribe to **Pro** for unlimited decks.

See [Billing & plans](/users/billing) for details.

### Option 2 — Free up a slot

Delete a deck you no longer need:

1. On the Dashboard, click the **Delete** icon on a deck.
2. Confirm deletion — all cards in that deck are removed permanently.
3. Click **New Deck** to create a replacement deck.

See [Decks](/users/features/decks) for edit and delete steps.

## Expected outcome

- **After upgrading:** The **X/3 decks used** banner disappears and you can create unlimited decks.
- **After deleting a deck:** Your count drops below 3 and **New Deck** works again.

## Still stuck?

- Confirm you are signed in to the account you expect.
- Refresh the page after upgrading — billing can take a moment to sync.
- If you upgraded but still see the limit, sign out and sign back in.

See [Sign-in issues](/users/troubleshooting/sign-in-issues) if you cannot access your account.
