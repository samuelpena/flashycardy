# Sign-in issues

If you cannot sign in, reach your Dashboard, or stay signed in, try the steps below.

## Sign-in modal does not open

1. **Disable popup blockers** for the Flashycardy site — sign-in opens in a modal overlay.
2. **Refresh the page** and click **Sign in** again.
3. Try a **different browser** (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge).

## Wrong account or empty Dashboard

You may be signed in to a different account than you expect.

1. Click your **profile picture** → **Sign out**.
2. Click **Sign in** and use the email you originally signed up with.
3. Check that your decks appear on the **Dashboard**.

Each account has its own decks — they are not shared between accounts.

## Signed in on web but extension or iOS shows signed out

Flashycardy clients share the same account but manage sessions separately on mobile and extension.

1. On the **Chrome extension**, open the side panel and sign in through the auth screen.
2. On **iOS**, tap **Sign in** on the landing screen.
3. On the **web extension**, signing in on the web app first may sync your session when you reopen the panel — if not, sign in directly in the extension.

Use the same email and password on every platform.

## Forgot password

1. Click **Sign in** on the home page.
2. Choose **Forgot password?** in the sign-in modal (wording may vary).
3. Follow the email reset link from your inbox.

If you signed up with a social provider (Google, Apple, etc.), use that provider's sign-in button instead of email and password.

## Session expires or signs out unexpectedly

1. Sign in again from the home page.
2. Clear site cookies for Flashycardy only if the problem persists, then sign in fresh.
3. Avoid private browsing if you need sessions to persist between visits.

## Error messages during sign-in

| Symptom | Try this |
|---------|----------|
| "Invalid credentials" | Double-check email and password, or use password reset. |
| Social sign-in fails | Ensure third-party cookies are allowed, or try email sign-in. |
| Page redirects to home in a loop | Clear cookies, sign in again, or try incognito to rule out extensions. |

## Expected outcome

After a successful sign-in, you reach the **Dashboard** and see your decks (or an empty state if you are new).
