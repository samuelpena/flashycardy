# Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to common Flashycardy questions.

## General

### What is Flashycardy?

Flashycardy is a flashcard app for creating decks, adding question-and-answer cards, studying them, and tracking your scores over time.

### Which platforms are supported?

Flashycardy is available on the **web**, as a **Chrome extension** (side panel), and on **iOS**. Sign in with the same account to access your decks on each platform.

### Is Flashycardy free?

Yes — the **Free plan** includes up to 3 decks, unlimited cards, study sessions, and analytics. **Pro** adds unlimited decks and advanced creation features. See [Billing & plans](/users/billing).

## Decks and cards

### How many decks can I create?

- **Free:** up to **3 decks**
- **Pro:** unlimited decks

### Is there a limit on cards per deck?

No. Add as many flashcards as you need within a deck.

### Can I recover a deleted deck or card?

No. Deleting a deck or card is permanent. Export or copy important content before deleting.

### Can I sort my decks or cards?

Yes. Both the Dashboard and deck detail pages offer sort options: **Last updated**, **A → Z**, and **Z → A**.

## Studying

### How do I start studying?

Open a deck and click **Study**. See [Study a deck](/users/workflows/study-a-deck).

### Are my study results saved automatically?

Yes. When you complete a session, Flashycardy saves your score. View history under **Analytics** on the Dashboard.

### What happens if I skip rating a card?

Skipped cards are noted in the session summary but do not count toward your correct/incorrect totals.

### Can I study the same deck multiple times?

Yes. Click **Restart** or **Shuffle & restart** at the end of a session, or start a new session anytime from the deck page.

## Account and billing

### How do I change the app language?

Open your profile → **Settings** → choose **English** or **Spanish** → **Save**. See [Account management](/users/account-management).

### How do I upgrade to Pro?

Open **Pricing** from the app header or click **View Plans** when you hit the deck limit. See [Billing & plans](/users/billing).

### How do I cancel Pro?

Open your profile → **Manage account** → use the billing section to manage or cancel your subscription.

## Troubleshooting

### I hit the 3-deck limit. What now?

Upgrade to Pro or delete a deck you no longer need. See [Deck limit reached](/users/troubleshooting/deck-limit-reached).

### I cannot sign in.

See [Sign-in issues](/users/troubleshooting/sign-in-issues).

### Where is the Help menu?

Click your **profile picture** → **Help**. It opens this documentation site.
