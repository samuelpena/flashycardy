# Welcome to Flashycardy

Flashycardy is a flashcard app for creating decks, studying cards, and tracking your progress across the web, Chrome extension, and iOS.

Detailed product documentation — features, workflows, billing, and troubleshooting — is being added in a follow-up release.

## Quick links

- Open the [Flashycardy web app](https://flashycardy.vercel.app/) to sign in or create an account
- From the **Dashboard**, click **New Deck** to create your first deck
- Open a deck and click **Study** to review cards
- Use **Pricing** in the app to compare Free and Pro plans

For technical integration docs, see the [Developer Guide](/developers/introduction) or [API Reference](/api/rest-api).
