# Billing & plans

Flashycardy offers a **Free** plan for getting started and a **Pro** plan with more capacity and advanced features. You manage your subscription through the in-app **Pricing** page.

## Free plan

The Free plan includes:

- Up to **3 decks**
- Unlimited flashcards per deck
- Manual card creation
- Study sessions and **Analytics**
- English and Spanish UI

When you approach the deck limit, the Dashboard shows **X/3 decks used**. If you try to create a fourth deck, Flashycardy explains the limit and offers **View Plans** to upgrade.

See [Deck limit reached](/users/troubleshooting/deck-limit-reached) if you hit this limit.

## Pro plan

Pro unlocks additional features, including:

- **Unlimited decks** — create as many decks as you need
- **Generate with AI** — add AI-generated flashcards to an existing deck (requires a deck description)
- **Create deck from document** — upload a PDF, Word, or PowerPoint file and get a deck with generated cards (web app)

Exact pricing and feature availability are shown on the **Pricing** page in the app, powered by Flashycardy's billing provider.

## How to upgrade

1. Click **Pricing** in the app header, or **View Plans** when you see a deck-limit dialog.
2. Review the available plans on the **Choose Your plan** page.
3. Follow the checkout steps to subscribe to **Pro**.

After upgrading, Pro features activate on your account immediately. You may need to refresh the page if a button still shows an upgrade prompt.

## How to manage your subscription

1. Click your **profile picture** in the top-right corner.
2. Select **Manage account**.
3. Use the billing section in your account settings to view invoices, update payment method, or cancel your subscription.

Cancellation details depend on your billing provider's policies shown in **Manage account**.

## Expected outcome

- **Free users** can create up to 3 decks and use all core study features.
- **Pro users** can create unlimited decks and access Pro-only creation tools when those features are enabled on their account.
