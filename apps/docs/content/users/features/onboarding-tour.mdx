# Onboarding tour

The first time you visit the **Dashboard** after signing up, Flashycardy may show a short **tooltip tour** pointing to the **New Deck** button.

## What it does

- Highlights where to create your first deck
- Offers **Finish** or **Skip**
- Runs once per browser — it does not repeat after you complete or skip it

## When you'll see it

- First visit to `/dashboard` on the **web app**
- Not shown on extension or iOS

## How to dismiss

- Click **Finish** when you understand the hint
- Click **Skip** to close without finishing

## Expected outcome

The tour closes and you can use the Dashboard normally. If you skipped it, use [Create your first deck](/users/workflows/create-your-first-deck) anytime.
