# Cards

A **card** is a single flashcard with two sides:

- **Front** — the question, term, or prompt
- **Back** — the answer or explanation

Cards live inside a deck. You can add as many cards as you need — there is no per-deck card limit.

## When to add cards manually

Add cards yourself when you already know what you want to study — vocabulary lists, exam facts, or notes you typed up. Manual entry gives you full control over every question and answer.

## View cards in a deck

1. Open a deck from the Dashboard.
2. Cards appear in a grid showing a preview of the **Front** and **Back**.
3. Use the sort menu to order cards by **Last updated**, **A → Z**, or **Z → A**.

If you have studied this deck before, each card may show **correct** and **incorrect** counts from past sessions — a quick hint for which cards need more practice.

## Add a card

1. On the deck detail page, click **Add Card**.
2. Enter the **Front** and **Back** text.
3. Click **Create Card**.

If the deck has no cards yet, click **Add your first card** instead — the steps are the same.

## Edit a card

1. Click the **Edit** icon on the card.
2. Update the **Front** or **Back**.
3. Click **Save changes**.

## Delete a card

1. Click the **Delete** icon on the card.
2. Confirm in the dialog.
3. The card is permanently removed from the deck.

## Expected outcome

New cards appear in the deck grid immediately. Edited cards show updated text. Deleted cards are gone from the deck and will not appear in future study sessions.
