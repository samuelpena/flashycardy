# Language settings

Flashycardy supports **English** and **Spanish** for buttons, menus, and messages.

## When to change language

Switch language if you prefer to use the app in Spanish (or back to English). Your choice applies across [platforms](/users/platforms/web) where you sign in with the same account.

## Steps (signed in)

### Web

1. Click your **profile picture** (top right).
2. Open **Settings** in the profile sidebar.
3. Under **Language**, choose **English** or **Spanish**.
4. Click **Save**.
5. The app refreshes with the new language.

### Chrome extension

1. Open the side panel and go to **Settings**.
2. Choose **English** or **Spanish**.
3. Save — preference syncs via your Clerk account.

### iOS

1. Open the **Settings** tab.
2. Change language and save.

## Expected outcome

Menus, buttons, and system messages appear in the selected language. Your decks and card content are **not** translated — only the app interface.

## Guest visitors (web only)

> **Note:** Changing language from the app UI requires a signed-in account. Visitors who are not signed in see English by default. There is no guest language picker on the web home page today.
