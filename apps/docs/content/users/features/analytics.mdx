# Analytics

**Analytics** shows a history of your completed **study sessions** — which deck you studied, your score, and when you finished.

## When to use it

Review Analytics when you want to track progress over time, compare sessions on the same deck, or confirm that study results were saved.

## Requirements

- Signed-in account
- At least one completed study session (see [Study a deck](/users/workflows/study-a-deck))

Available on **web**, **Chrome extension**, and **iOS**.

## Steps

### Web

1. From the **Dashboard**, click **Analytics** in the header.
2. Browse the table of sessions.

### Chrome extension

1. Open the side panel.
2. Navigate to **Analytics** (via the app navigation).

### iOS

1. Open the **Analytics** tab in the bottom tab bar.

## What each column means

| Column | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Deck** | Which deck you studied (tap/click to open it on web) |
| **Total Cards** | Cards in that session |
| **Correct** / **Incorrect** | How you rated cards |
| **Score** | Percentage correct (color-coded) |
| **Completed At** | When the session finished |

## Expected outcome

Every completed session appears in the list. If you just studied and do not see a row, wait a moment — results save at session end. An empty state prompts you to study a deck first.
