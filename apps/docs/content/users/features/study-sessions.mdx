# Study sessions

A **study session** is a run through the flashcards in a deck. You flip each card, mark whether you got it right, and see a score when you finish. Flashycardy saves your results so you can track progress over time.

## When to study

Study whenever you want to review material — daily refreshers, pre-exam cramming, or spaced repetition. Open any deck that has at least one card and click **Study**.

## How a session works

1. **Start** — From the deck detail page, click **Study**.
2. **Flip** — Read the front, then click the card or press **Space** to reveal the back.
3. **Rate** — After flipping, choose **Got it** (you knew the answer) or **Nope** (you did not).
4. **Navigate** — Use **Previous** / **Next** or the arrow keys (← →) to move between cards.
5. **Finish** — Complete all cards to see your session summary.

During a session you can also:

- **Shuffle** — randomize card order
- **Restart** — begin again from the first card

## Session summary

When you finish, Flashycardy shows:

- **Correct** and **Incorrect** counts
- **Score** — percentage of rated cards you got right
- A note if any cards were **skipped** without a rating

Results save automatically. You do not need to click a separate save button.

## View your history

Open **Analytics** from the Dashboard to see a table of all completed study sessions, including:

- Which deck you studied
- Total cards, correct, and incorrect counts
- Score percentage
- When the session completed

Click a deck name in the table to jump back to that deck.

## Keyboard shortcuts

| Action | Shortcut |
|--------|----------|
| Flip card | **Space** or click the card |
| Previous card | **←** |
| Next card | **→** |

## Expected outcome

After a session, your score is saved and visible under **Analytics**. Per-card correct/incorrect counts update on the deck detail page, helping you spot weak areas on your next visit.
