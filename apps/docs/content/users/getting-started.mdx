# Getting started

This guide walks you through signing up, creating a deck, adding cards, and studying — everything you need for your first session.

## 1. Create an account

1. Open the Flashycardy web app.
2. On the home page, click **Sign up**.
3. Complete the sign-up form in the modal that appears.
4. After sign-up, you are taken to your **Dashboard** — your personal deck library.

Already have an account? Click **Sign in** instead.

{/* screenshot: home-sign-in-sign-up */}

## 2. Create your first deck

1. On the Dashboard, click **New Deck** (or **Create your first deck** if your library is empty).
2. Enter a **Name** — for example, "Spanish Vocabulary" or "Biology Chapter 5".
3. Optionally add a **Description** — a short note about what the deck covers.
4. Click **Create deck**.

Your new deck appears on the Dashboard. Click it to open the deck detail page.

See also: [Create your first deck](/users/workflows/create-your-first-deck) for a focused walkthrough.

{/* screenshot: create-deck-dialog */}

## 3. Add flashcards

1. Open your deck from the Dashboard.
2. Click **Add Card** (or **Add your first card** if the deck is empty).
3. Enter the **Front** — the question or prompt you want to remember.
4. Enter the **Back** — the answer or explanation.
5. Click **Create Card**.

Repeat for each flashcard you want in the deck. There is no limit on cards per deck.

{/* screenshot: add-card-dialog */}

## 4. Study your deck

1. On the deck detail page, click **Study**.
2. Read the front of the card, then click the card or press **Space** to flip it.
3. Mark **Got it** if you knew the answer, or **Nope** if you did not.
4. Continue through all cards until the session completes.
5. Review your score summary — correct count, incorrect count, and percentage.

Your results are saved automatically. View them later under **Analytics** on the Dashboard.

See also: [Study a deck](/users/workflows/study-a-deck) for keyboard shortcuts and tips.

{/* screenshot: study-session-in-progress */}

## 5. Explore the Dashboard

From the Dashboard you can:

- **Sort** decks by last updated, A → Z, or Z → A
- **Edit** or **delete** a deck using the icons on each deck card
- Open **Analytics** to see your study history
- Check how many decks you have used on the Free plan (shown as **X/3 decks used**)
