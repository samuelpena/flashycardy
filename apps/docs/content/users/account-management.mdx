# Account management

Your Flashycardy account is managed through the profile menu in the top-right corner of the web app. The same account works on the web, Chrome extension, and iOS when you sign in with the same credentials.

## Sign in and sign up

On the Flashycardy home page:

- Click **Sign in** to open the sign-in modal.
- Click **Sign up** to create a new account.

Both open in a modal — you stay on the same page. After signing in, you are redirected to your **Dashboard**.

## Profile menu

Click your **profile picture** to open the menu:

| Option | What it does |
|--------|----------------|
| **Manage account** | Open account settings — email, password, connected accounts, and billing |
| **Help** | Open this documentation site |
| **Sign out** | Sign out of Flashycardy on this device |

## Settings (language)

Flashycardy supports **English** and **Spanish** for the app interface.

1. Click your **profile picture**.
2. Select **Settings** in the profile sidebar (or open Settings from the profile pages).
3. Choose **English** or **Spanish** under **Language**.
4. Click **Save**.

The app refreshes with your selected language. Your preference is stored on your account and applies wherever you sign in.

> **Note:** Language settings require a signed-in account. Guest visitors always see English until they sign in and choose a language.

## Manage account (Clerk)

**Manage account** opens your account portal where you can:

- Update your email address or password
- Manage connected sign-in methods
- View billing and subscription details (if on Pro)

Changes here apply to your Flashycardy identity across all platforms.

## Sign out

Click **Sign out** in the profile menu to end your session on the current device. Your decks and study history remain saved — sign back in anytime to continue.
