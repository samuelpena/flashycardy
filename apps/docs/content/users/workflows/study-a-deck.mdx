# Study a deck

Use this workflow whenever you want to review the flashcards in a deck and record your score.

## Before you begin

- The deck must have at least one card. If it is empty, [add cards](/users/features/cards) first.
- You must be signed in.

## Steps

### 1. Open the deck

From the **Dashboard**, click the deck you want to study.

### 2. Start studying

Click **Study** on the deck detail page.

### 3. Work through each card

For each card:

1. Read the **front** side.
2. Try to recall the answer before flipping.
3. Click the card or press **Space** to reveal the **back**.
4. Tap **Got it** if you knew the answer, or **Nope** if you did not.
5. Move to the next card with **Next** or the **→** key.

Use **Previous** or **←** if you need to go back.

{/* screenshot: study-got-it-nope-buttons */}

### 4. Use shuffle or restart (optional)

- **Shuffle** — randomizes the remaining order mid-session or before restart.
- **Restart** — starts over from the first card without leaving the page.

### 5. Review your summary

When you finish the last card, Flashycardy shows your **Session complete** summary:

- How many cards you got **Correct** vs **Incorrect**
- Your **Score** as a percentage
- Any cards you skipped without rating

Results save automatically.

### 6. Study again or return

- Click **Shuffle & restart** or **Restart** for another round.
- Click **Back to decks** (or use the back link) to return to the Dashboard.

## Tips for effective study

- Rate honestly — **Got it** only when you truly knew the answer without peeking.
- Skipped cards do not count toward your score but are noted in the summary.
- Check **Analytics** on the Dashboard after several sessions to spot trends.

## Expected outcome

Your session appears in **Analytics** with the deck name, score, and completion time. Cards you marked incorrect may show higher incorrect counts on the deck detail page next time you visit.

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| **Study** button missing or page says no cards | Add at least one card to the deck. |
| Results not appearing in Analytics | Wait a moment — saving happens at session end. If you see an error on the summary screen, try studying again while signed in. |
| Session feels too long | Use **Shuffle** and study a subset by restarting after a portion, or split content into multiple decks. |
