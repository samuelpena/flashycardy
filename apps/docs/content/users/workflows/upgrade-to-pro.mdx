# Upgrade to Pro

Move from the Free plan to **Pro** to unlock unlimited decks and AI-powered features.

## What Pro adds

| Feature | Free | Pro |
|---------|------|-----|
| Decks | Up to **3** | **Unlimited** |
| Manual cards & study | Yes | Yes |
| Analytics | Yes | Yes |
| **Generate with AI** on existing deck | No | Yes |
| **Import from document** (web) | No | Yes |
| **Generate from web page** (extension) | No | Yes |

Details: [Billing & plans](/users/billing).

## Steps

1. Click **Pricing** in the app header, or **View Plans** when you hit a deck limit.
2. Review plans on the **Choose Your plan** page.
3. Complete checkout with Clerk billing.
4. Return to the app — Pro features activate on your account (refresh if needed).

### From a specific upsell

| You saw… | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Deck limit dialog | Click **View Plans** |
| **Generate with AI** upgrade button | Click it — goes to Pricing |
| Missing **Document** tab on New Deck | Open **Pricing** from header |

## Expected outcome

- **X/3 decks used** banner disappears on Dashboard
- **Generate with AI** works on decks with descriptions
- **Document** tab appears in **New Deck** (web)
- **Generate from this page** works in extension (Pro)
