# Create your first deck

This workflow takes you from an empty Dashboard to a deck ready for flashcards.

## Before you begin

- You need a Flashycardy account. If you are not signed in, see [Sign-in issues](/users/troubleshooting/sign-in-issues).
- On the **Free plan**, you can create up to **3 decks**. See [Billing & plans](/users/billing) if you need more.

## Steps

### 1. Open the Dashboard

Sign in to Flashycardy. You land on the **Dashboard** — titled **Your Decks**.

If this is your first visit, a short tour may point you to the **New Deck** button. You can finish or skip the tour.

{/* screenshot: dashboard-tour-new-deck */}

### 2. Start the create dialog

Click **New Deck** in the header (or **Create your first deck** in the empty state).

### 3. Name your deck

Enter a clear **Name** — this is the title you will see on the Dashboard and when studying.

Examples:

- "Medical Terminology — Unit 2"
- "JavaScript Array Methods"
- "Capitals of Europe"

Optionally add a **Description** — a sentence about what the deck covers. Descriptions help you remember why you created the deck later.

### 4. Create the deck

Click **Create deck**. The dialog closes and your deck appears on the Dashboard.

### 5. Add your first cards

1. Click the new deck to open it.
2. Click **Add your first card**.
3. Fill in **Front** and **Back**, then click **Create Card**.
4. Add at least a few cards so your first study session is meaningful.

### 6. Study (optional)

When you are ready, click **Study** on the deck detail page. See [Study a deck](/users/workflows/study-a-deck).

## Expected outcome

You have one deck on your Dashboard with at least one card. The deck count banner (if on Free) shows **1/3 decks used**.

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| **New Deck** is disabled or shows a limit message | You may have 3 decks already. See [Deck limit reached](/users/troubleshooting/deck-limit-reached). |
| Dialog does not open | Refresh the page and sign in again. See [Sign-in issues](/users/troubleshooting/sign-in-issues). |
| Deck created but not visible | Check pagination at the bottom of the Dashboard — you may be on another page. |
